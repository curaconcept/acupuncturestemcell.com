Additional Information

Faradarmani is a holistic healing modality based on the principles of consciousness, energy, and the interconnectedness of the mind and body. Faradarmani is considered a complementary or alternative therapy and aims to facilitate self-healing by accessing the individual's inherent abilities.

The word "Faradarmani" translates to "The Art of Consciousness and Energy." According to its proponents, Faradarmani works on the principle that each person possesses an innate healing power within themselves. The practice focuses on activating and harnessing this self-healing ability to address physical, emotional, and psychological imbalances.

In Faradarmani, the practitioner acts as a facilitator rather than a healer, creating a supportive environment for the individual to connect with their inner healing resources. The practice does not involve physical contact or the use of any tools or substances. Instead, it emphasizes intention, attention, and the power of consciousness.

During a Faradarmani session, the practitioner may guide the individual to access their subconscious mind, explore their emotions, and become aware of their thoughts and beliefs. By doing so, it is believed that the individual can identify and release energetic blockages or imbalances that contribute to illness or disharmony. The process aims to promote self-awareness, self-empowerment, and a deeper understanding of oneself.

Patients can feel the scanning as pain, a spasm, a sensation of hot or cold, of seeing color or lights, feeling sore, an itch, etc., with a session typically lasting 20 to 30 minutes.
